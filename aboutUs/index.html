About ParkSwap

Built for the moment a parking spot opens.

ParkSwap helps drivers share real-time street-parking information and coordinate a clearer handoff between the person leaving and the person arriving.

01 / Who we are

Driver-powered

We are building a community where drivers can help one another find on-street parking with less circling, uncertainty, and wasted time.

02 / What we do

Coordinate the handoff

A safely parked driver shares a spot, one nearby driver reserves it, and both sides receive the status needed to complete the arrival.

03 / Why it matters

Make cities easier

Street parking is a daily source of stress in dense cities. Better timing and clearer information can make the experience calmer for everyone.
